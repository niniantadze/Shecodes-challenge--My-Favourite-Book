My Year of Rest and Relaxation

Ottessa Moshfegh

From one of our boldest, most celebrated new literary voices, a novel about a young woman‚Äôs efforts to duck the ills of the world by embarking on an extended hibernation with the help of one of the worst psychiatrists in the annals of literature and the battery of medicines she prescribes.

Ottessa Moshfegh is a fiction writer from New England.
